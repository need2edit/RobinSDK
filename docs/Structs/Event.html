Event

public struct Event: Serializable

A meeting or reservation, booked in a space.

Events are often synchronized from an external calendar service, such as Google Calendar or Office 365. Events may also be created by booking a Space through Robin.

  • uid

    Event unique identifier as defined in RFC5545. It is used to uniquely identify events across calendaring systems. Note that the UID and the id are not identical. One difference in their semantics is that in recurring events, all occurrences of one event have different ids while they all share the same UIDs.
