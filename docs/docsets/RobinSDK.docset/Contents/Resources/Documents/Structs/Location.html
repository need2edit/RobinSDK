Location

public struct Location: Serializable, ImageRepresentable, ChangeTrackingRecord, LocationRepresentable

A collection of spaces. Most commonly a building or floor.

Physically speaking, a location is typically an office or a building and can be associated to a specific physical address.

Warning

Locations should exist on a map. Locations that do not adhere to this constraint by containing spaces that belong to different (or non-existent) geographical locations may be subject to removal.
